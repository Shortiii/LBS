Our Benefits

These are what alumni gain from using the directory

  1. 1. Reconnect with former classmate
  2. 2. Find alumni in similar industries
  3. 3. ⁠Expand professional networks
  4. 4. ⁠Discover mentors or mentees
  5. 5. ⁠Collaborate on business or community initiatives

What’s Inside the Directory

This is a brief overview of what users will find

  1. 1. Alumni profiles (name, year, department, job info)
  2. 2. Search and filter tools
  3. 3. ⁠Location-based search
  4. 4. ⁠Messaging or contact options (if available)

How to Search for other Alumni

1

Open the Alumni Directory and log in.

2

Use the main search bar to type a name, graduation year, department, or program.

3

Apply advanced filters (optional) such as industry, job title, location, or mentorship availability.

4

Review the results and select a profile to see further details.

5

⁠Connect using available options (message, email, or LinkedIn), depending on the alumnus’s privacy settings.

6

If you cannot find someone, try using fewer keywords or contact the Alumni Office for assistance.
