Stories That Inspire. Voices That Lead.

Simi Adeagbo

November 18, 2025

Share

The Global Impact Initiative at Lagos Business School (LBS) is making significant strides in providing sustainable solutions across Africa. Through collaborative research, innovative programs, and strategic partnerships, LBS is addressing critical challenges in areas such as healthcare, education, and environmental sustainability.

Recent projects include developing affordable housing solutions using eco-friendly materials, implementing mobile health clinics in rural communities, and creating educational programs focused on renewable energy. These initiatives not only improve the quality of life for local populations but also contribute to long-term economic growth and stability.

LBS is committed to fostering a culture of social responsibility among its students and faculty, encouraging them to become leaders in sustainable development. By integrating sustainability into its curriculum and research, LBS aims to empower the next generation of business leaders to create positive change in Africa and beyond.
